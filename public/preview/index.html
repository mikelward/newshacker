Things to weigh:

  • White surface: the outer ring has zero contrast against a white tab strip — it disappears, and the icon reads identical to the no-ring variant. So the ring earns its keep only on darker surfaces.
  • Dark surface: the ring becomes a halo, which can read either as "designed" or as "stuck inside a sticker." Subjective.
  • Maskable PWA icon (Android adaptive): the OS crops to a circle/squircle with a ~40% safe zone. An outer ring at the canvas edge will get clipped on aggressive crops. The no-ring variant is unaffected.
  • Coherence with the in-header mark: mono-a's in-header mark is a filled orange disc with no ring — exactly the no-ring variant. Picking the outer-ring icon means the favicon and the in-header mark tell slightly different stories.
